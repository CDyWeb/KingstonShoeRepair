Home

 

Kingston Shoe Repair and Orthopedic Shoe Service is ready to serve you at 51 Montreal Street, just off Princess Street in beautiful downtown Kingston.

 

We pride ourselves on first class repairs and alterations of footwear. Whether it's general shoe repair, adding lifts or modifying footwear- we can do it. We also carry a basic selection of common shoe care products and offer key cutting services.

 

Kingston Shoe Repair is owned and operated by Rodney Sothmann who, aside from working in the shoe repair, orthopedic alteration and custom footwear industry, has had several years of education and experience as an orthotic and prosthetic technician. Rodney draws on these diverse experiences to provide Kingston with a unique service.
